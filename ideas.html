Data project ideas and datasets

This list has some ideas for your projects, and some links to datasets. I have not checked these links. In some cases, there are duplicate datasets with different links, just in case one link doesn’t work.

DISCLAIMER: this is not a carefully curated dataset. Rather, it is a starting point full of stuff that I put together very quickly. I will add to it as time goes on, and if I happen to see that a link isn't working then I will remove it.

Please keep your message in the form short. If you want to send a longer message, please use my email addresses at the bottom of this page.

Any URLs submitted will be verified prior to adding to the website.

Use the form below to recommend an idea or dataset!











Real estate

  • Predicting Housing Prices: Build a machine learning model to predict the prices of houses based on various features such as location, size, and amenities.
  • Real Estate Analytics: Analyze real estate data such as property values, rental prices, or vacancy rates to gain insights into the housing market and make recommendations for investment strategies.

Data:

  • Advanced Regression Techniques (Kaggle)
  • Zillow Prize: Zillow’s Home Value Prediction (Zillow)
  • Boston Housing Dataset (UCI Machine Learning Repository)

Customers / retail / marketing

  • Customer Segmentation: Use clustering techniques to segment customers based on their purchasing behavior and demographics to help businesses identify target segments and personalize marketing strategies.
  • Marketing Campaign Optimization: Use machine learning algorithms to optimize marketing campaigns by identifying the best channels, messages, and targeting strategies for different customer segments.
  • Retail Analytics: Analyze retail data such as sales transactions, inventory, or customer behavior to gain insights into consumer trends and make recommendations for product placement and pricing strategies.
  • Marketing Attribution: Analyze marketing data to identify the impact of different marketing channels and campaigns on customer acquisition and retention.
  • Customer Lifetime Value Prediction: Use machine learning techniques to predict the expected revenue generated by a customer over their lifetime, which can help businesses make decisions about marketing strategies and customer retention.
  • Demand Forecasting: Build a machine learning model to predict the demand for a product or service based on historical sales data and external factors such as seasonality or promotions.
  • Customer Churn Prediction: Use machine learning algorithms to predict which customers are likely to churn or cancel their subscription based on their usage patterns and behavior.

Data:

  • Telco Customer Churn dataset
  • Walmart Sales Forecasting dataset
  • Mall Customer Segmentation Data
  • Online Retail Data Set (UCI Machine Learning Repository)
  • Customer Segmentation Data (Kaggle)
  • Online Retail Dataset

Text / sentiment

  • Sentiment Analysis: Analyze customer reviews or social media data to determine the sentiment of a product or brand and identify areas for improvement.
  • Natural Language Processing: Use NLP techniques to analyze and extract insights from large amounts of text data such as news articles, social media posts, or customer feedback.

Data:

  • Amazon Reviews Dataset
  • Sentiment140 Dataset
  • Amazon Product Reviews dataset
  • Amazon Reviews dataset (UCI Machine Learning Repository)
  • Twitter US Airline Sentiment (Kaggle)
  • IMDB Reviews Dataset (Kaggle)
  • Reuters News Corpus
  • Amazon Fine Food Reviews dataset
  • The Large Movie Review Dataset (IMDB)
  • The Enron Email Dataset (CMU)
  • Yelp Reviews Dataset

Fraud detection / cyber security

  • Fraud Detection: Use machine learning algorithms to detect fraudulent activities in financial transactions or insurance claims.
  • Cybersecurity Analytics: Analyze cybersecurity data such as network traffic, user behavior, or logs to detect and prevent cyber attacks.
  • Anomaly Detection: Use machine learning algorithms to detect unusual patterns or anomalies in data such as network traffic, financial transactions, or medical data.

Data:

  • Numenta Anomaly Benchmark dataset
  • KDD Cup 1999 Dataset
  • Insurance Fraud Detection
  • Credit Card Fraud Detection (Kaggle)
  • IEEE-CIS Fraud Detection (Kaggle)
  • Synthetic Financial Datasets For Fraud Detection (Kaggle)

Recommendation systems

  • Recommendation Systems: Build a recommendation system to suggest products or content to users based on their past behavior or preferences.
  • Music Recommendation System: Build a recommendation system that suggests music to users based on their listening history, mood, and preferences.
  • Recommendation for Health Products: Build a recommendation system to suggest health products to users based on their health history, lifestyle, and behavior.
  • Travel Recommendation System: Build a recommendation system that suggests travel destinations and activities to users based on their preferences, budget, and travel history.

Data:

Health analytics

  • Food and Nutrition Analytics: Analyze nutrition data such as food intake, health history, or genetic data to identify trends and patterns and make recommendations for personalized nutrition plans.
  • Healthcare Analytics: Analyze healthcare data such as electronic health records, clinical trials, or medical imaging to identify patterns and make predictions about patient outcomes and treatment efficacy.

Transportation

  • Traffic Prediction: Build a machine learning model to predict traffic patterns and congestion based on historical traffic data and external factors such as weather or events.
  • Smart City Analytics: Analyze data from smart city sensors such as traffic cameras, air quality sensors, or waste management systems to optimize urban planning and improve quality of life.
  • Transportation Analytics: Analyze transportation data such as traffic patterns, public transportation usage, or ride-sharing data to gain insights into transportation behavior and make recommendations for infrastructure improvements.

  • Climate Change Mitigation: Analyze energy consumption data to identify areas where energy can be saved and make recommendations for reducing carbon emissions.
  • Natural Disaster Prediction: Build a machine learning model to predict natural disasters such as earthquakes, floods, or wildfires based on historical data and environmental factors.

Data:

  • Supply Chain Optimization: Use machine learning algorithms to optimize the supply chain by predicting demand, managing inventory, and improving logistics.
